## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click the **Register** button in the top-right corner of the forum header.
2. Fill in your details: email address, username, and password.
3. Click **Register** to create your account.
4. You will be logged in automatically after registration.

### Logging In

1. Click the **Login** button in the top-right corner.
2. Enter your email/username and password.
3. Click **Login**.

### Forgot Password

1. On the login form, click **Forgot Password?**
2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. Check your email for a password reset link.
4. Follow the link to set a new password.

## Navigating the Forum

The forum has a three-column layout:

- **Left Sidebar** - Navigation links to all main sections: Questions, Tags, Users, Saved, Messages, Activity, Guidelines, and Report an Issue.
- **Main Content Area** - The primary content for the current page.
- **Right Sidebar** - Displays trending/hot questions for quick access.

### Main Sections

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Questions | Browse and search all forum questions |
| Tags | Browse, follow, and manage topic tags |
| Users | View community members and manage connections |
| Saved | Your bookmarked/saved threads |
| Messages | Private messaging with other members |
| Activity | Your personal activity history |
| Guidelines | Community rules and guidelines |
| Report Issue | Submit a report to moderators |

## Questions & Threads

### Browsing Questions

The main **Questions** page shows all forum threads. You can filter questions using the tabs at the top:

- **Newest** - Most recently posted questions (default).
- **Active** - Questions with recent activity (new replies).
- **Unanswered** - Questions with no replies yet.
- **Recommended** - Questions recommended for you.
- **More** dropdown (logged-in users):
  - **Mine** - Questions you have posted.
  - **Frequent** - Most frequently viewed questions.
  - **Score** - Highest-scoring questions.

### Layout Options

Click the **three dots button** next to the filter tabs to switch between:

- **Card** - Full view with title, excerpt, tags, and stats (default).
- **Compact** - Condensed view without the excerpt text.

Your layout preference is saved in the browser and remembered for future visits.

### Searching Questions

Use the **Search** bar below the filter tabs to search across question titles, content, and replies. Results update as you type. Matching text is highlighted in the results.

### Asking a New Question

1. Click **New Question** on the Questions page, or navigate to **Ask Question** from the sidebar.
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Title** - A clear, concise summary of your question.
   - **Content** - Detailed description of your question.
   - **Category** - Select an existing category or create a new one.
   - **Tags** - Add up to 10 tags to help others find your question. You can select existing tags or create new ones.
3. **Anonymous posting** - Toggle the **Anonymous** switch if you want to post without revealing your identity. Your name and avatar will be hidden.
4. Click **Post Question**.

> **Note:** Depending on forum settings, your question may need moderator approval before it becomes visible to others.

### Viewing a Thread

Click on any question title to open the full thread page. Here you can:

- Read the complete question and all replies.
- Vote on the question (upvote or downvote).
- Mark it as helpful (save it).
- View the total number of views.
- Post a reply.
- Report the thread if it violates guidelines.

### Thread Badges

- **Pinned** - This thread has been pinned to the top by a moderator.
- **Closed** - This thread has been closed and is no longer accepting replies.

### Editing or Deleting Your Thread

If you are the author of a thread:

1. On the thread detail page, look for the **Edit** and **Delete** buttons near the top.
2. **Edit** opens a modal where you can update the title and content.
3. **Delete** asks for confirmation before permanently removing the thread.

## Replies & Discussions

### Posting a Reply

1. Scroll to the bottom of a thread page to find the reply box.
2. Type your reply in the text area.
3. Click **Reply** or press **Ctrl+Enter** to submit.

You can also expand replies inline on the Questions page by clicking the **Replies** button on any question card, then use the reply box that appears.

> **Note:** If a thread is closed, you will see a notice: "This thread has been closed by a moderator and is no longer accepting replies." The reply box will not be available.

### Nested Replies

You can reply directly to a specific reply to create a threaded conversation:

1. Click the **Reply** button on any existing reply.
2. A reply box appears below that specific reply.
3. Your reply will be indented under the parent reply, showing the conversation thread.

### Anonymous Replies

Toggle the **Anonymous** option when posting a reply to hide your identity. Your name and avatar will be replaced with an anonymous indicator.

### Editing or Deleting Your Reply

- Click the **Edit** icon on your reply to modify it.
- Click the **Delete** icon to remove it (confirmation required).

### Best Answer / Solution

The thread author (or a moderator) can mark a reply as the **Solution**:

- Click the **Mark as Solution** button on the best reply.
- The reply will be highlighted and the thread will be marked as "answered."
- This awards reputation points to the reply author.

## Voting & Helpful Marks

### Voting on Questions and Replies

Each question and reply has **Upvote** and **Downvote** buttons:

- **Upvote** - Click the thumbs-up icon to indicate the content is useful.
- **Downvote** - Click the thumbs-down icon to indicate the content is not useful.
- Click the same vote button again to remove your vote.
- Vote counts are displayed next to each button.

Voting affects the author's reputation score.

### Marking as Helpful

Click the **Helpful** (heart) button on any question to save it and indicate you found it helpful. The helpful count is displayed publicly. Click again to remove the mark.

## Messaging

### Accessing Messages

Click **Messages** in the left sidebar to open the messaging interface.

### Sending a Message

**From the Messages page:**

1. Click the **compose** (pencil) icon at the top of the messages panel.
2. Search for a user by name in the "To:" field.
3. Select the user from the results or suggested users list.
4. Type your message in the input bar at the bottom.
5. Click **Send** or press **Enter**.

**From a User's Profile:**

1. Visit the user's profile page.
2. Click the **Message** button.
3. You will be redirected to the Messages page with that user selected.

> **Note:** The Message button only appears if the user's privacy settings allow you to message them. If they have set their message privacy to "Connections Only," you must be connected to them first.

### Message Features

- **Attachments** - Click the attachment icon to upload files or images along with your message.
- **Pinned Messages** - Click the pin icon on important messages to pin them for easy access later.
- **Typing Indicator** - When the other person is typing, you will see a "typing..." indicator.
- **Read Receipts** - A checkmark appears when your message has been read.
- **Online Status** - A green dot next to a user's avatar indicates they are currently online.

### Managing Conversations

In the right panel of the messages view, you can:

- **Search messages** within the current conversation.
- **View pinned messages** for quick reference.
- **Browse media gallery** to see all shared images.
- **View shared files** to see all shared documents.
- **Mute** a conversation to stop receiving notifications.
- **Restrict** a user to prevent them from messaging you.
- **Remove connection** to disconnect from the user.
- **Block** a user to prevent all interaction.

## Connections

### What Are Connections?

Connections are mutual relationships between users, similar to "friends." Being connected may grant additional privileges such as:

- Messaging users who have set their privacy to "Connections Only."
- Viewing profiles that are restricted to connections only.

### Sending a Connection Request

1. Visit a user's profile page.
2. Click the **Connect** button.
3. The button changes to "Request Sent" while pending.

### Accepting or Declining Requests

**From the Users page:**

1. Go to **Users** in the sidebar.
2. Click the **Requests** tab to see pending incoming requests.
3. Click **Accept** to connect, or **Decline** to reject.

**From a User's Profile:**

When viewing the profile of someone who sent you a request:

1. You will see **Accept Request** and **Decline** buttons.
2. If their profile is restricted to connections only, these buttons still appear within the restricted notice area.

### Removing a Connection

1. Visit the connected user's profile.
2. The connection options will be available in the profile actions area.
3. You can also remove connections from the Messages panel.

## Tags

### Browsing Tags

Navigate to **Tags** in the sidebar to see all available tags. Filter using:

- **All Tags** - Every tag in the forum.
- **My Tags** - Tags you created.
- **Following** - Tags you follow.
- **Not Following** - Tags you have not followed yet.

Use the search bar to find specific tags.

### Following Tags

Click **Follow** on any tag card to follow it. Following tags helps you stay updated on topics you care about. Click **Unfollow** to stop following.

### Creating Tags

You can create new tags when asking a question:

1. In the tag field, type a tag name that does not exist.
2. A "Create tag" option will appear.
3. Click it to create and add the tag.

You can also create tags from the Tags page if this feature is available.

### Tag Details

Each tag card shows:

- **Tag name**
- **Question count** - How many questions use this tag.
- **Description** - What the tag is about.

## Your Profile

### Viewing Your Profile

Click your avatar or name in the top-right corner and select your profile, or navigate to your profile from the Users section.

### Profile Tabs

Your profile has several tabs:

| Tab | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| Answers | Replies you have posted |
| Questions | Threads you have created |
| Saved | Threads you have bookmarked |
| Activity | Your complete activity history |
| Warnings & Suspensions | Any moderation actions on your account |
| Stats | Your forum statistics |
| Settings | Privacy and notification preferences |

### Editing Your Profile

On your own profile:

1. Click **Edit** to modify your profile information.
2. You can update: First name, Last name, Email, Phone number, Professional Title, and Verified Label.
3. Click **Save** to apply changes.

### Uploading an Avatar

1. On your profile page, click the camera icon on your avatar.
2. Select an image file from your device.
3. The avatar is uploaded and updated immediately.

### Anonymous Mode

Toggle the **Anonymous** switch on your profile to make all your future posts anonymous by default. When anonymous:

- Your name and avatar are hidden on your threads and replies.
- An anonymous icon is shown instead.
- Your reputation is still tracked internally.

## Notifications

### Viewing Notifications

Click the **bell icon** in the header to open the notifications dropdown. A badge shows the number of unread notifications.

### Notification Types

You will receive notifications for:

- **Replies** - Someone replied to your thread.
- **Votes** - Someone upvoted or downvoted your content.
- **Solutions** - Your reply was marked as the best answer.
- **Connections** - Someone sent, accepted, or declined a connection request.
- **Mentions** - Someone mentioned you.
- **Messages** - You received a new direct message.

### Managing Notifications

- Click on a notification to navigate to the related content.
- Unread notifications have a blue dot indicator.
- Click **Mark all read** to clear all unread indicators.

### Email Notifications

Email digests are sent periodically based on your settings. Configure email preferences in your Profile Settings (see Privacy & Settings section below).

## Reporting Issues

### Submitting a Report

If you encounter content that violates community guidelines:

1. **From a thread or reply:** Click the **Report** (flag) button and fill in the report form.
2. **From the Report page:** Navigate to **Report an Issue** in the sidebar.

### Report Form

Fill in the following:

- **Report Type** - Select the category:
  - Inappropriate Content
  - Harassment or Bullying
  - Spam or Self-Promotion
  - Confidentiality Violation
  - Misinformation or Harmful Advice
  - Technical Issues / Bugs
  - Other
- **Subject** - Brief summary of the issue.
- **Description** - Detailed explanation.
- **Link** (optional) - URL to the specific content.

### What Happens After Reporting

Your report is submitted to the moderation team. A moderator will:

1. Review your report.
2. Investigate the reported content.
3. Take appropriate action (warning, content removal, suspension, etc.).
4. Update the report status.

You are not notified of the outcome to protect all parties involved.

## Moderation (Moderators)

If you have been assigned the **Moderator** role, you have access to additional features.

### Accessing the Moderation Panel

Navigate to **Moderation** in the sidebar. The moderation dashboard shows:

- **Open** - Reports awaiting review.
- **In Progress** - Reports being investigated.
- **Warnings** - Active warnings issued.
- **Suspensions** - Currently suspended users.
- **Closed** - Resolved reports.

### Managing Reports

1. Click the **Reports** tab to see all submitted reports.
2. Filter by status: Open, In Progress, Resolved, Closed.
3. Click on a report to view details.
4. Add moderator notes.
5. Change the report status as you work through it.

### Thread Moderation

On any thread detail page, moderators can:

- **Pin/Unpin** - Pin important threads to the top of the question list.
- **Close/Reopen** - Close threads to prevent further replies, or reopen them.
- **Edit** any thread's title and content.
- **Delete** any thread.
- **Approve/Reject** pending threads.

### Reply Moderation

Moderators can:

- **Edit** any reply.
- **Delete** any reply.
- **Mark as Solution** on any reply.
- **Approve/Reject** pending replies.

### Issuing Warnings

1. Go to the **Take Action** tab in the Moderation panel.
2. Search for and select the user.
3. Enter the reason for the warning.
4. Click **Issue Warning**.

The user will see the warning on their profile under the Warnings & Suspensions tab.

### Suspending Users

1. Go to the **Take Action** tab.
2. Search for and select the user.
3. Choose suspension type:
   - **Temporary** - Specify number of days.
   - **Permanent** - Indefinite suspension.
4. Enter the reason.
5. Click **Suspend User**.

Suspended users cannot post threads, replies, or send messages while the suspension is active. Temporary suspensions are automatically lifted when the duration expires.

## Privacy & Settings

### Accessing Settings

1. Go to your profile page.
2. Click the **Settings** tab.

### Privacy Settings

| Setting | Options | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Profile Visibility | Everyone / Connections Only | Controls who can view your full profile. When set to "Connections Only," non-connected users see a restricted view. |
| Message Privacy | Everyone / Connections Only | Controls who can send you direct messages. Default is "Connections Only." |
| Show Online Status | On / Off | When off, other users will not see your online/offline status. Changes take effect immediately for users currently viewing your status. |
| Show Activity | On / Off | Controls whether your activity history is visible to others. |

### Notification Preferences

Toggle notifications on or off for each type:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Reply Notifications | Notify when someone replies to your thread |
| Vote Notifications | Notify when someone votes on your content |
| Solution Notifications | Notify when your reply is marked as the solution |
| Connection Notifications | Notify on connection requests and responses |
| Mention Notifications | Notify when someone mentions you |
| Message Notifications | Notify on new direct messages |

### Email Notification Mode

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Always | Receive email digests regardless of your online status |
| When Inactive | Only receive email digests when you are offline |
| Never | Do not receive any email notifications |

### Saving Settings

After making changes, click **Save Settings**. A confirmation message appears when settings are saved successfully.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why can't I see the Message button on someone's profile?

The user has set their message privacy to "Connections Only." Send them a connection request first. Once they accept, the Message button will appear.

### Why does my post say "Pending"?

The forum may require moderator approval for new threads or replies. Your post will become visible once a moderator approves it.

### Can I change my anonymous status after posting?

Anonymous status is set at the time of posting and cannot be changed afterward for that specific post.

### How do I increase my reputation?

Reputation points are earned by:
- Posting questions and replies
- Receiving upvotes on your content
- Having your reply marked as the solution
- Being active in the community

### Why was I suspended?

Check the **Warnings & Suspensions** tab on your profile for details. If you believe the suspension was issued in error, contact the forum administrator.

### How do I delete my account?

Contact the forum administrator to request account deletion.
